--------------------------
Game Instructions
--------------------------

Survive waves of evil robot bugs for 5 minutes while your mechs charge up.  Use the turrets or go out on foot and fight them.  When your mechs have charged up - use them to jump out of the base and escape to the dropship before its too late!

--------
Controls 
--------

wasd - move your character/robot
e - board a turret via a console or board a robot after its been charged
space - jump your character/robot (robots can jump out of base)
mouse - point your mouse cursor at things you want to shoot
left mouse - shoot your weapon
left shift - run
